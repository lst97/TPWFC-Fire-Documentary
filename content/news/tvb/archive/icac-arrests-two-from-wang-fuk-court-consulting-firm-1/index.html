The Independent Commission Against Corruption has arrested two people, who are reportedly the heads of the consulting firm responsible for the maintenance work at Wang Fuk Court in Tai Po.

ICAC officers escorted one of the suspects to the office of Will Power Architects in Kowloon Bay this afternoon to search for evidence. He was then taken to ICAC headquarters in a private car for further investigation. 

Earlier, ICAC officers escorted another suspect to his office to search for evidence. 

The ICAC previously said it had established a special task force to conduct a full investigation into whether corruption was involved in the Wang Fuk Court maintenance work.
