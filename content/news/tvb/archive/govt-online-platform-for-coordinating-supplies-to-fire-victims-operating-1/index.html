Gov't online platform for coordinating supplies to fire victims operating

Gov't online platform for coordinating supplies to fire victims operating

發佈日期: 2025-12-01 12:08
TVB News
已複製連結
The online platform for donating supplies, built by the government together with the information technology sector, began operating yesterday.

The government stated that organizations and members of the public can register the supplies they can donate on the platform, so that the government can consolidate the information centrally and distribute items as needed to residents affected by the fire at Wang Fuk Court in Tai Po.

The government has set up a central supplies station at the Tai Po Community Centre to coordinate and properly manage the donated materials.
